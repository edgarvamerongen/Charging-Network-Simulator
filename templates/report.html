{# ===================== ABOUT NRG2FLY ==================================== #}
NRG2FLY

About NRG2fly

NRG2fly is a Dutch company dedicated to the charging infrastructure that electric aviation needs to get off the ground. We help airports across Europe prepare for the first electric aircraft and the rotations that follow — working alongside aircraft manufacturers, grid partners and regional stakeholders.

Consulting

Electrification strategy, demand modelling, and the roadmap from a single charger to a full energy hub.

Hardware

Selecting and integrating the right chargers for aircraft, matched to your aircraft mix and grid connection.

CPO & MSP

Operating the charging points and the mobility-service layer, so charging is reliable from day one.

What this report means for your airport

This document translates a concrete flight schedule into the energy and power your airport would need to serve it — kWh per day, peak kW, the number of chargers, and how rotations fill the operating day. The figures are indicative — a strategic sizing aid, not a procurement specification. Its real value is as a starting point for a conversation about the airport as an energy hub: a site that not only charges aircraft, but balances peaks, integrates local generation and storage, and serves ground vehicles from the same infrastructure.
