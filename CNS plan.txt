NRG2FLY

What we do:
Electric aviation strategic planning
GSE (ground support equipment) electrification
Renewably energy integration 
Energy Hubs
Charging stations + solutions

4 planes:
- Pepistrel Velis Electro: 	2 seater, 22kWh, 100km range
- Beta plane: 			4-6 seater (~500kg load), 225kWh, 500km range
- Vearidion: 			9-30 seater (~3000kg load), 500kWh, +500km range


Charging: 
- Aircraft charger: 		250A 172kW
- GSE charger:			180kW
- Mobile Aircraft charger:	22kW GB/T
- DC Mobile Charger:		40kW / 40kWh

Charging Technologies:
CCS: 				max. 400kW
MCS:				3,75MW output


goal: Charging network simulator
We want to calculate power demand for a given trajectory and airplane + load. This is presented in a visualisation tool. Build datasets with the planes, charging options and charging technologies

variables: 
- distance from airport A to airport B
- departures a day
- plane of choice (we can build custom planes) (battery cap. and flight speed)

calculate:
- power needed to arrive at destination 
- turnaround time (how long to recharge the battery to make it back to the original point of departure)
- flight time

What should the application do:
- let the user pick two European airports
- visualize the flight using OpenStreetMap
- it calculates a crude prediction of the usage 
- it can provide a simulation/mode
- turnaround calculation (energy E [kWh]required per aircraft per flight / charing power P [kW])
